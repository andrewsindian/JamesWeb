Professor Dr K C James Raju 

Brief Biodata

2009- present Professor of Electronic Science at School of Physics, University of Hyderabad (UoH), Hyderabad, India.

2006- 2009 Associate Professor of Electronic Science at School of Physics, UoH.

2003- 2006 Reader of Electronic Science at School of Physics, UoH

2001- 2003 Senior Lecturer of Electronic Science at School of Physics, UoH.

1995-PhD-in Microwave Materials and Devices. From Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Madras, 

Title of Thesis: Correlations between Microwave Dielectric Resonator Materials and Ferroelectrics in (Ba,Sr)(Zr,Ti)O3 system.

1995-1996 Post Doctoral Fellow, Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, India.

1996- 2001 Lecturer of Electronic Science at School of Physics, UoH

1986- MSc -MG University- India


Awards & Fellowships:
